### About
**ARC Traders** is a simple website for item trade listings for ARC Raiders.  
The goal is to keep it straightforward: you post a listing with an item image, describe what you offer and what you want, and leave a contact (Steam/Discord). Deals happen directly between users.

### Open source and non-profit
This is a community-made, open source project and it is non-profit.  
It is not official and has no affiliation with Embark Studios, Nexon, or any company related to the game.

### Basic rules
- Post only trade-related content.
- You are responsible for the information you share in your listing.
- Trades happen outside the website, so please use common sense and stay safe.
